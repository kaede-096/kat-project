Game Play

Real World and SEKAI

Move between two worlds and listen to what the characters are saying!

Enjoy the story with fully voiced lines including Miku and VIRTUAL SINGERS!
*VIRTUAL SINGERS are partially voiced in some parts

Rhythm Game System

Tap, hold, and flick your way to the end to reach the highest score; Earn EXP and various items to level up your characters and unlock outfits.

Select your own unit members and play through shows with 6 difficulties to choose from!

A whole library of 3DMVs! Customize the video by changing characters and costumes!

Ranked Matches

Get to Rank 20 and have at least 5 full combos on expert or above to compete!
Climb the ladder and receive fantastic rewards!
